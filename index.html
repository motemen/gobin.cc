gobin.cc is a curated list/short URLs of 'go get'-able programs.

You can 'go get' one by: 

go get gobin.cc/PROGNAME

For more information, visit repository.

Available packages are:

  • gobin.cc/reflex (github.com/cespare/reflex)
  • gobin.cc/golint (github.com/golang/lint/golint)
  • gobin.cc/mockgen (github.com/golang/mock/mockgen)
  • gobin.cc/go-bindata (github.com/jteeuwen/go-bindata/go-bindata)
  • gobin.cc/govendor (github.com/kardianos/govendor)
  • gobin.cc/gox (github.com/mitchellh/gox)
  • gobin.cc/gore (github.com/motemen/gore)
  • gobin.cc/gocode (github.com/nsf/gocode)
  • gobin.cc/ghr (github.com/tcnksm/ghr)
